Company Profile

The business name, iMaps can be interpreted in various ways. The letter “I” pertains to “intelligence” on the other hand “maps” is a diagram or other visual representation that shows the relative position of the parts of something. The iMaps Enterprises was founded in 2018 by Justine Carlo P. Esteban and colleagues that are from students of Department of Hotel and Tourism Management in Far Eastern University. This business introduces the innovation of board games that have relation in traveling through cultural mapping and geography of the Philippines.
