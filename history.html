Our History

The iMaps Enterprise is managed by Tourism students from the Far Eastern University and founded on 2018. The company goals is to bring about a product or service that is related to the Tourism Industry. The company aims to produce a product that will help to promote and acknowledge the importance of culture, history, tradition, and background of a particular place. It introduces a product which is having a relation in traveling through cultural mapping-designed which features backgrounds of a particular geography.
